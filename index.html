You push the door gingerly and it swings open with ease, a blast of repulsive aroma streams past you and some green haze drifted from the room out of sight. “Mom” you whisper.

There is no answer there.

You sneak into the workshop and close the door fluently. You have repeated these actions hundreds of times whenever your Witch mom is not at home.

Shelves crammed with glass jars of weird ingredients; a black pointed hat rests on a shabby broomstick; several cauldrons are still bubbling and steaming with self-stirring spoons.

You don’t know why she constantly bars you from learning witchery. Witchery is so compelling, you cannot prevent yourself from involving into this magical world. And your belief never change, you are destined to become a famous wizard.

You find out a worn witchery book, read it, then turn to a brewing cauldron.

You are going to make something magic.
